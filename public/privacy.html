Privacy Policy

A starting draft, please have it reviewed before launch.

1. What we collect

Account details you give us (name, email, login), billing details handled by our payment provider, and usage data (searches you run, mockups and sites you create, features you use). We also process public business information you search for.

2. How we use it

To provide and improve the service, process payments, give you support, keep the product secure, and understand how it is used so we can make it better.

3. Lawful basis and your responsibilities

When you contact businesses you find through Site Pounce, you act as the data controller for that outreach and are responsible for complying with UK GDPR, PECR and the rules of any channel you use (email, SMS, WhatsApp, phone).

4. Sharing

We use trusted providers to run the service (for example hosting, authentication, payments, email and AI). They process data on our behalf under agreements. We do not sell your personal data.

5. Retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. Search results are retained for a limited period (currently 45 days) and then removed. You can request deletion of your account data.

6. Your rights

You can access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and object to certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Contact

Email ajay@aimpro.co.uk for any privacy question or request.

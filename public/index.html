Cold WhatsApp messages to people who never opted in are against WhatsApp's rules and get numbers restricted, even when you press send yourself. Use the 📞 Call List (or SMS/email) for first contact, and keep WhatsApp for people who have already replied or clicked.

👥 Team

Add a colleague from your company. They sign in with their own email and share this workspace (your leads, call list, searches and mockups), free on your plan. Tick what they are allowed to do. Suspend to pause access, or remove them entirely.

What can they do?

After you add someone, they go to sitepounce.com and sign up with that exact email, then they are straight into your shared workspace. Server actions (searching, mockups, websites, call list) are enforced; the rest are hidden in their app.

🚦 Limits

How many searches, mockups, websites and lead research runs each person can do in a rolling window. Each person has their own allowance, so one busy day cannot lock anyone else out. Leave a box blank to use the default.

These are cost guards: searches call the Google Places API and mockups and websites call the AI image service, so every one of them costs real money. Raise them as far as you need, just know your bill follows.
